Trainline are one of the biggest train ticket operators in the UK and needed a mobile offering that was built from the user up.


I joined the Trainline mobile team at the start of 2015, when they already had apps in both the iOS App Store and the Google Play Store, but lacked customer satisfaction with an average rating of 2 stars.

  • Include

  • Principal Product Designer
  • Are a keen problem solver - you enjoy thinking through complex product challenges and care about solving the right problem
  • Have a well trained eye for great visual design execution. You care about getting things right - one pixel off is a big deal.
  • You’re comfortable with ambiguity
  • Take a strategic view to everything you do - your work stands the test of time
  • Are fantastic at communicating your thinking and design decisions - you’re able to get people along on the journey with you
  • Are well versed at working with disciplines in your work to achieve the best outcome, such as user research, content and engineering.
  • Enjoy mentoring others in their professional development as designers
  • Have a great appetite (we love eating!)
  • Have a good sense of humour!

  • Cookpad

  • Worldwide – Indonisia, Russia, Easten cultures
  • Diverse users, cultures and traditions
  • Concept to delivery
  • Mobile apps and web platform
  • Work closely with design/product teams
  • Design standards, documentation
  • Shaping product strategy
  • consistency to all the platforms and products
  • Design culture, hiring, inspiring
  • Mentor, coaching and working through design thinking together
  • Learning away from desk
